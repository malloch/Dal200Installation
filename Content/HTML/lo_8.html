EMPLOYMENT

Dr. Nur Zincir-Heywood is a Full Professor of Computer Science at Dalhousie University.

RESEARCH

Her work focuses on computer networks, autonomous systems, cybersecurity and privacy. She has a strong background in networking, and intelligent systems. With her students, she works on network information management and security systems using AI and machine learning approaches.

AWARDS

She is a recipient of the 2017 Women Leaders in the Digital Economy Award and is the Technical Program Co-chair of the Traffic Measurement and Analysis Conference 2018.
